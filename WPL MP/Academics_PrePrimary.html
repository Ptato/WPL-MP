Pre-Primary

“Starting preschool is a milestone in your child’s life. Your child is embarking on a journey that will lead him / her on many paths of discovery and learning. Brain development is highest during the first six years of life. Hence it is important to provide an environment that is rich in experiences, and where the child can explore, experiment and make discoveries for himself through various activities, books, stories, poems, dramas, shows, field trips, toys etc.”

Through our project method we help children develop intellectually, emotionally, socially and physically in preparation for formal schooling. By the time your child completes pre-primary, he/she will develop a sound base of academic fundamentals and will become more organized and independent. We have a wonderful group of trained and experienced teachers and support staff giving motherly care and love to children. We realize that teaching requires an enormous amount of enthusiasm, commitment and patience. We choose to put our children’s interest above all. We choose to be creative, innovative and accept that children have different learning styles. We choose to create an environment which is warm and nurturing. We have a kitchen garden and a play ground with modern play equipments. We celebrate all festivals regardless of caste and creed to inculcate a feeling of nationalism and unity among the children.
