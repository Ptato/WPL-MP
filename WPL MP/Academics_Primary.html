Primary

The Primary section focuses keenly on developing varied skills in children that assist in life long learning. Today, the school is proud of having a team of able, dedicated teachers who leave no stone unturned in developing the intellectual, language, social, emotional, aesthetic and physical growth of the students in their care.

To ensure the achievement of above, several activities which includes Sports, Karate, Table Tennis, Abacus, Music, Bharat Natyam, yoga, English Oral Vidyapeeth Exam for std. 1st to 3rd and Middle School Scholarship for std. 4th are conducted in school throughout the year, as part of curriculum transaction and for co-scholastic areas. These activities are conducted to build confidence in students, varied and focus on the basic development of the child.

Orientation programme of parents are conducted at the start of the year to brief them on the implementation of the syllabi and the varied activities that are conducted during the year.

Updates of student performance is done through Parent-Teacher meetings that are held during the course of the year. A number of awareness drivers viz. Cleanliness, First Aid, Importance of Plants and Trees, Eating Healthy Food etc. are held from time to time.Selected students are formally invested as Prefects and assist in the maintenance of discipline and smooth functioning of the school.

Class and special assemblies help create an awareness of the importance of days viz. Republic and Independence Day, Children’s and Teacher’s Day, Gandhi Jayanti, Diwali, Eid, Holi, Christmas etc.

School stands proud in many of its achievements and continues to strive in its mission of creating independent and responsible students.
